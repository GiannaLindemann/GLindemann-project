Project Video Tutorials

Videos and hands on demonstrations can be extremely helpful before diving into a project. Seeing the full process play out allows us to learn by seeing, by asking questions, and then by doing as you create the project with your own personal twist. Watching the project be done and then creating an example project for your class also helps ensure you have all the necessary supplies gathered an organized logically following the sequence of the steps involved. All of the videos on this page come from art teachers art teachers who have a passion for their work, give clear explanations and encourage enthusiastic creativity.
