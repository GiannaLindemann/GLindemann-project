Art Across the Ages

While each student offers different strengths and challenges there are developmental stages and themes that can be helpful to note and engage with to enrich teaching strategies.

Grade Level Age Strengths Focus Areas
K/1/2 5-8 years Less self judgement and baggage around artistic talen Enthusiasm Interesting abstract creations Simple Color Theory, color mixing Identifying and creating shapes Cleanup and self awareness
3/4 8-10 years Ability to describe artwork Interest in 3D building Teamwork and sharing developed Investing in long term projects New mediums such as collage, origami
5/6 10-12 years Independent studio time Quickly grasp new concepts Art as a form of self expression, relaxation & source of pride
